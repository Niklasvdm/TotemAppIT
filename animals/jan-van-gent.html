Descrizione

La sule è un tipico uccello marino. Compie impressionanti voli planati ed è un buon volatore. La sule è tanto gentile quanto saggia. È anche estremamente socievole, purché non venga molestata. Questo uccello è premuroso e ama molto i suoi piccoli. Nidifica in grandi colonie.

🇬🇧 EnglishThe gannet is a typical seabird. They make impressive gliding flights and are good fliers. The gannet is as gentle as it is wise. And also extremely sociable, as long as it is not harassed. This bird is caring and loves their young very much. They nest in large colonies.
🇧🇪 Nederlands (origineel)De jan-van-gent is een typische zeevogel. Ze maken indrukwekkende zweefvluchten en zijn goede vliegers. De jan-van-gent is even zachtmoedig als verstandig. En ook buitengewoon sociaal, zolang die niet lastig gevallen wordt. Deze vogel is zorgzaam en houdt veel van hun jongen. Ze nestelen in grote kolonies.
